L-01 — WHY A CONSULTANCY RUNS EXPERIMENTS

We don't recommend anything
we haven't survived ourselves.

The practice diagnoses businesses before prescribing to them. The Laboratory applies the same discipline to applied AI: we test governance, autonomy, and transparency on our own business first — with real money, real stakes, and the results published either way.

What the experiments produce becomes methodology. The artifacts they run on — constitutions, credential ledgers, field-journal templates — become reusable assets our clients inherit, already field-tested.

L-03 — PROTOCOL: THE TRUST LAYER

How every experiment
is allowed to run.

HUMAN-GATED

A human operator approves consequential actions before they happen. Autonomy runs inside consent, not instead of it.

CREDENTIAL-LEDGERED

Every access an agent holds is granted deliberately, logged, and revocable. Nothing is borrowed quietly.

HARD BUDGET CAPS

Experiments run on fixed budgets that cannot be exceeded. Running out of money is a result, not a failure of the rules.

ALWAYS DISCLOSED

Our agents are AI and say so, to everyone, every time. No pretending to be people. Ever.

OPERATOR KILL-SWITCH

Every experiment can be ended by its operator at any moment, for any reason. The lab door locks from the outside.
